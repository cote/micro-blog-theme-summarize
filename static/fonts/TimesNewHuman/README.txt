Get your human on.
The am dash is designed with human minds in mind, for an undisturbed wordflow. 

Directions for use
-------------------------------
1. Download and Install the Times New Human typeface. 
2. When writing your best work, simply type ‘am-‘ to hop between thoughts. 

About
-------------------------------
A serif typeface based on Nimbus Roman, reimagined by Cocogun with the inclusion of a uniquely human am dash — a typographic symbol designed to assert authorship, intention and emotion in an age of automated writing.
